About Us

The Army Drama Group is a passionate collective of performers dedicated to inspiring faith and igniting hope through the power of storytelling. With dynamic performances that blend drama, emotion, and spirituality, the group brings biblical truths and uplifting messages to life on stage. Every performance is crafted to connect with audiences on a profound level, leaving a lasting impact and drawing hearts closer to faith. Join us on this journey of artistic expression and spiritual reflection, where every act is a call to action and every story is a reminder of hope
